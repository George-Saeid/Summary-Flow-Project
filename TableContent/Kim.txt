“Oh ye who tread the Narrow Way By Tophet-flare to Judgement
Day, Be gentle when the heathen pray To Buddha at Kamakura!”
He sat, in defiance of municipal orders, astride the gun Zam-
Zammah on her brick platform opposite the old Ajaib-Gher- the
Wonder House, as the natives call the Lahore Museum.
